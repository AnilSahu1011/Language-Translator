How to Use This Platform

Procedure


Step-1
Search at the address bar then search the the website Language Translator. Click Here
Step-2
Click the Translator option from the menu bar, It opens the Translator page.
Step-3
Type your script then choose your script language from dropdown menu, then choose your required/destination language from the right side dropdown menu. Then click the "Let's Go" Button. The Output will be show within 2 to 3 second.
